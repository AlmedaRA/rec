Keanu Reeves


Who is he


Keanu Charles Reeves, was born on September 2, 1964 in Beirut Lebanon, and raised in Toronto Canada. He was known for his famous roles and movies such as John Wick From the Johnathan "John" Wick Franchise, Theodore "Ted" Logan from Bill & Ted, & Thomas "Neo" Anderson from The Matrix series.



His Early Carrer


In a Chinese-Hawaiian Heritage on his geologis father's side, Reeves' first name "Keanu" translates to "cool breeze over the mountains". His mother Worked as a performer in a entertainment service and a costume designer later. When Reeves' parents splitted he moved out together with his sister and mother to New York and then Toronto. Reeves had a big interest for Hockey, which made him a actor and started his big-screen debut in the 1985 Canidian feature "One Step Away".


Other interests


Reeves was also from a band called Dogstar in the early 1990s. He also played a bass for a band called Becky. He was also a motorcycle enthusiast, he had his own custom-built bike designed by Gard Hollinger back in 2011. Reeves also helped care for his sister who was battling against leukemiya, and supported other organizations to fight against Cancer and PETA.


Movies


Reeves becmame famous for his Action-Thriller film called "John Wick" and a sci-fi action film called "The Matrix". He also played as a motorcycle stunt man named "Duke Kaboom" from the "Toy story 4". Reeves appered as himself in a Netflix 2019 Rom-com film called "Always be my Maybe". Reeves played as a supernatural exorcist named John Constantine in a 2005 Horror film "Constantine".
